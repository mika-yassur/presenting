projecting topographies

Through what lenses are colonizing viewpoints projected?

How are these viewpoints inserted into landscapes?

What is retained? Translated? Reappropriated? Digested? Pixelated? Blurred? Reshaped? Censored?

How is the landscape reformed to correspond to these projections?

I am interested in exploring materialities, creative cartographies, topographical lines, ecological transformations (can fermentation be political? can burning? can growth?), through art, investigation, repetition, writing, mapping, and sensing of all kinds.

More specifically I am interested in GIS, composites of satellite images, LiDAR, projectors, the interplay between the digital and non digital.
